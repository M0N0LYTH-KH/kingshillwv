Meet Kings Hill

Born in the Islands.
Built in Weirton.

Kings Hill was founded with a simple idea: local businesses in the Northern Panhandle deserve the same quality of creative and marketing services that big-city companies take for granted.

Growing up connected to St. Croix, U.S. Virgin Islands, I learned that community, pride, and showing up for each other are everything. That spirit is baked into every project we take on.

Whether I'm building a marketing automation system that runs while you sleep, or spending a Saturday morning photographing your restaurant before the lunch rush, the mission is always the same — help good local businesses grow.

Our Inspiration

The Name: Kings Hill

Named for the historic Kings Hill neighborhood in St. Croix, U.S. Virgin Islands — a place of elevation, community, and cultural pride. We carry that energy into every business we serve here in Weirton and the Northern Panhandle.
